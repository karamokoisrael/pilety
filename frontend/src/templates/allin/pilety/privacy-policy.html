Privacy Policy for Pilety

At Pilety, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our clients and their personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information.

Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information from our clients in order to provide our transport, logistics, warehousing, and product sourcing services. This information may include your name, contact information, payment information, and other relevant details. We may collect this information through our website, email, phone, or in person.

Use of Personal Information

We use your personal information to provide our services to you. This includes processing payments, arranging transportation and logistics, managing warehousing, and sourcing products. We may also use your information to communicate with you about our services and respond to any inquiries or requests you may have.

Disclosure of Personal Information

We will not disclose your personal information to any third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • When necessary to protect our rights or property
  • When necessary to prevent fraud or other illegal activities
  • When necessary to provide our services to you (e.g. sharing your contact information with a transportation provider)
